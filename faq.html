Frequently Asked Questions

  • What is CareMate?
    CareMate is an AI-powered health assistant that helps users understand their symptoms and prepare for healthcare visits by providing instant analysis and trusted medical resources.
  • Is CareMate a replacement for a doctor?
    No. CareMate provides general health information and symptom analysis, but it does not replace professional medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Always consult a healthcare provider for serious or persistent symptoms.
  • How does CareMate analyze symptoms?
    CareMate uses advanced algorithms and up-to-date medical data to compare your symptoms with known conditions and provide possible matches and advice.
  • Is my data safe?
    Yes. Your symptom data is stored securely and never shared with third parties. You can clear your history at any time. See our Privacy Policy for details.
  • Can I use CareMate on my phone?
    Absolutely! CareMate is designed to work on all modern devices, including smartphones, tablets, and desktops.
  • How do I contact support?
    You can email us at support@caremate.health or use the contact details on our website.
  • Is CareMate free?
    Yes, CareMate is free to use for personal health symptom analysis.

CareMate does not provide medical advice. For emergencies, contact your local healthcare provider immediately.
