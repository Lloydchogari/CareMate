Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

Your privacy is important to us. CareMate is committed to protecting your personal information and ensuring transparency about how we use your data.

2. What Information We Collect

  • Symptom Data: Information you enter about your symptoms.
  • Usage Data: Anonymous analytics about how you use CareMate.
  • Contact Information: Only if you contact us for support.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide symptom analysis and health guidance.
  • To improve our services and user experience.
  • To respond to your support requests.

4. Data Protection

We use industry-standard security measures to protect your data. Your symptom entries are not shared with third parties and are stored securely.

5. Data Retention

Symptom data is stored only for as long as needed to provide our service. You can clear your history at any time.

6. Your Rights

  • Access, update, or delete your data at any time.
  • Contact us for any privacy concerns: support@caremate.health

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page.

8. Contact

For questions about privacy, email us at support@caremate.health.

CareMate does not provide medical advice. For emergencies, contact your local healthcare provider immediately.
